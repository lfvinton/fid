Community Farm Project

Helping communities increase self-sufficiency and food security

Program Basics Eligibility Resources

Healthy food and food access initiatives are most successful when those communities most impacted are not only active participants, but also leaders in the transformation efforts. The Community Farm Project competitive grants program awards grants to eligible nonprofits, tribal organizations, and food program service providers to promote self-sufficiency and increase food security in low-income communities by developing comprehensive, community-based solutions. Projects vary in scope, ranging from community gardens with market stands to marketing and consumer cooperatives, but all must involve low-income participants.
